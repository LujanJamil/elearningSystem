Our Most Popular Courses

Quran and Yasarna Studies

This course unlocks the secrets of the Quran and deepen your understanding of Allah's word. This comprehensive course includes learning Yasarna, Quran Memorisation, Recitation, Surah by Surah analysis and Tafsir(Quran translation).

IPLE Cirricullum

This course combines Islamic theology and secular education, covering subjects like Quran, Arabic language, Sharia (Islamic law), and Hadith (teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)).

Enroll Now
Arabic Language

This course will take you from beginner to advanced levels, covering reading, writing, conversation and comprehension lessons and also Improve your communication skills, enhance your understanding of Islamic texts, and connect with the rich cultural heritage of the Arab world.

Enroll Now
Idaad Syllabus (O-Level)

The course holds subjects like Quranic recitation, Hadith studies, Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh), Arabic language, alongside general academic subjects like mathematics, science, and English with a standard examination usually taken at the end of secondary school (Senior Four).

Enroll Now
Thanawi Syllabus (A-Level)

In this course we'll heavily emphasizes on Islamic subjects like Quranic exegesis, Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh), Hadith studies, and Arabic language proficiency at advanced level. After passind this course you'll gain entry into Islamic universities and other programs within mainstream universities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Islamic learning platform?

Our Islamic learning platform is an online education platform that provides Islamic courses, lectures, and resources for Muslims around the world.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply click on your course choice then click the "Enroll Now" button. You will be asked to create an account or log in if you already have one.

What is the cost of the courses?

Our courses have favourable subscriptions that provides all features and benefits you would expect.

How do I access my course materials?

Once you have enrolled in a course, you can access your course materials by logging in to your account and clicking on my class to find your dashboard.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the course?

No, we don't offer money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course, you can request a more lessons and resources within 30 days of enrollment.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by calling us, sending us a whatsapp message or emailing us at support@samaislamicdigitalplatform.com or by using the contact form on our website.
